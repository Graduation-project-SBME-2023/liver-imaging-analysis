About liver segmentation

Liver segmentation helps in accurately identifying and diagnosing liver diseases such as liver cancer, cirrhosis, and liver abscesses. Accurate diagnosis leads to better treatment planning and improved patient outcomes.

AI-based liver segmentation algorithms can analyze medical images and segment the liver and lesions much faster than manual methods. This can save a significant amount of time for radiologists and other medical professionals, allowing them to focus on other critical tasks.

Workflow

Upload 3D volume

Upload 3D volume for the abdomen. Use nifti extention (.nii or nii.gz)

Display the results

The results will be shown as 2 GIFs, the original and segmented volume.The segmented volume will contain the liver in purple color and the lesions in red color. You can display the volume in the 3 views (axial, coronal and sagittal) using drop menu. In addition, you will be able to pause, play, jump forward and jump backward the GIFs for better examination.

Tumors visualization

Click on "visualize tumors" button to examine the tumors found in the volume. Navigate through the tumors found using the drop menu and choose your suitable display mode (zoom, box, contour).
